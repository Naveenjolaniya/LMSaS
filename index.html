Documentation

This section discusses about the algorithm followed in designing subject allocation

Step 1

Every faculty member has to fill the subject and the priority score.The priority score filled carries a weightage of 50 percent of the overall score.

Step 2

The faculty has to enter the total number of times he/she has taught the subject.This parameter carries a weightage of 20 percent of the overall score.

Step 3

The faculty has to enter the highest degree achieved by him/her. Higher the level of degree, higher is the score given.This parameter accounts for 30 percent of the overall score.
